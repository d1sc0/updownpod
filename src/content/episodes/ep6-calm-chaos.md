Today was crowded with meetings, errands, and unexpected admin, but I felt oddly steady through it all. The trick was batching context switches and keeping one simple checklist open all day.

In the evening I reviewed ideas from [Cal Newport](https://calnewport.com/) and trimmed my commitments for next week. Fewer priorities means better execution, not less ambition.

- Grouped all errands into one route
- Used one list for personal and work tasks
- Closed the day with a short journal entry

> "Clarity is kindness to your future self."
