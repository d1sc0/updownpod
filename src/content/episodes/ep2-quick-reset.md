I took a deliberate pause today and walked the long way home after reading [this note on slowing down](https://zenhabits.net/slow/). It felt like switching tabs in my brain from chaos to clarity.

Later, I sat with coffee and reviewed my tasks using the simple framework from [Getting Things Done](https://gettingthingsdone.com/). Two pages of scribbles became one tidy plan for tomorrow.

- 30 minute walk before lunch
- One focused work block with notifications off
- Phone-free evening for proper rest

> "You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems."
