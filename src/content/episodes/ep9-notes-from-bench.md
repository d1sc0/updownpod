I took my notebook to a park bench instead of opening my laptop, and thinking got easier immediately. Fewer tabs, more attention, better questions.

While there, I revisited [Austin Kleon's blog](https://austinkleon.com/) and an old favorite from [Seth's Blog](https://seths.blog/). Both reminded me that making small things often is better than waiting for perfect ideas.

- Wrote one page by hand
- Outlined two short episode concepts
- Planned tomorrow's priority before leaving
