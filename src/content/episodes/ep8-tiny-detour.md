I missed a turn on my walk and ended up by the water with no agenda, which felt like a lucky mistake. Sometimes the best moments come from not trying to optimize every minute.

I listened to an episode from [99% Invisible](https://99percentinvisible.org/) and then saved a few ideas in [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/). The detour gave me better notes than a planned desk session.

- 40 minute walk with no fixed route
- Captured three ideas for future episodes
- Sent one message I had been delaying

> "Leave room for serendipity."
