The weather turned dramatic just before sunset, so naturally I went out for a soggy loop anyway. I followed a route inspired by [Komoot](https://www.komoot.com/) and managed to dodge only half the puddles.

Afterward, I ducked into a pub and read a few pages from [The Almanack of Naval Ravikant](https://www.navalmanack.com/). The rain, the noise, and the warm room somehow made everything feel less urgent.

- Easy 7km run in steady rain
- Stretch session while listening to old playlists
- One page of reflections before bed

> "A calm mind, a fit body, a house full of love."
