I tried a tiny experiment today: one mile run, one flat white, one notebook page. It sounds almost too simple, but the structure worked better than any complicated productivity system.

The route came from [Strava](https://www.strava.com/) ideas nearby, and I ended up at a cafe recommended by [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/). Movement plus caffeine remains undefeated.

- One mile at an easy pace
- Ten minute planning sprint at the cafe
- Zero doom-scrolling before noon
