I started the day by finishing two boring tasks I had postponed all week, and that instantly improved my mood. There is something satisfying about clearing tiny blockers before they become huge.

At lunch I read [James Clear's piece on habits](https://jamesclear.com/atomic-habits) and then checked my own weekly notes in [Notion](https://www.notion.so/). The big lesson: consistency beats intensity when life gets noisy.

- Replied to every pending message
- Updated my running plan for the month
- Scheduled one meaningful catch-up call
