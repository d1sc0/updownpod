I overslept, missed my usual routine, and started the day feeling behind. Instead of spiraling, I used a short reset from [Headspace](https://www.headspace.com/) and focused on the next right step.

By afternoon I had recovered momentum and finished more than expected. I also bookmarked a practical article on [time blocking](https://todoist.com/productivity-methods/time-blocking) that I want to test this week.

- 15 minute reset walk without headphones
- Two deep-work sessions with clear boundaries
- Early shutdown to protect tomorrow's energy
