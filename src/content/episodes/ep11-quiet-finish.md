I wrapped the week with a quiet review session and a long walk at dusk. No dramatic breakthroughs, just clean closure and a better starting point for Monday.

I used [Trello](https://trello.com/) to archive finished tasks and checked a short guide on [weekly reviews](https://todoist.com/productivity-methods/weekly-review). The process was simple and surprisingly calming.

- Archived done tasks and cleared inboxes
- Picked three priorities for next week
- Set up one non-negotiable recovery block
